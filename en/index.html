An authentic experience in the heart of the Atlas

Imsfrane Cathédrale is a remarkable mountain formation located near Tilouguite in Azilal Province.

The region combines spectacular landscapes, Oued Ahansal, Amazigh culture and unforgettable adventures.

  • Local knowledge and authentic hospitality
  • Activities for couples, families and groups
